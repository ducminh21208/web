Commands

These are all the chat commands that are available.

To send a private message to a player, you can click on their name in chat, or use any of these commands:

  • /msg [name]
  • /whisper [name]
  • /pm [name]
  • /w [name]

You can quickly reply to another player's private message with

  • /r [message]
  • /reply [message]
    Be aware that, this sends your message to the player that last messaged you. You may accidentally send your message to the wrong player.
    If you want to be absolutely sure, instead use the following, which sends your message to the player that you last messaged.
