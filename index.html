Commands

These are all the chat commands that are available.

To send a private message to a player, you can click on their name in chat, or use any of these commands:

  • /msg [name]
  • /whisper [name]
  • /pm [name]
  • /w [name]

You can quickly reply to another player's private message with

  • /r [message]
  • /reply [message]
    Be aware that, this sends your message to the player that last messaged you. You may accidentally send your message to the wrong player.
    If you want to be absolutely sure, instead use the following, which sends your message to the player that you last messaged.
  • /l [message]
  • /last [message]

Hiding specific player messages

  • /ignore [name]
    This will ignore a player temporarily, and is cleared periodically.

  • /ignorehard [name]
    This will ignore a player permanently, saved to your ignore list.
    You can repeat this command with the playername you wish to unignore.

  • /ignorelist
    This will display a list of players you have ignored permanently.
    You can click on the button next to a player's name to unignore them.

  • /ignoredeathmsgs [name]
    This will hide a player's death messages.

Chat visibility

  • /togglechat
    This will toggle the visibility of the default global chat.

  • /toggleprivatemsgs
    This will toggle the visibility of private messages.

  • /toggledeathmsgs
    This will toggle the visibility of death messages.

Miscellaneous

  • /kill
    Instantly kills your player.

  • /queue
    Displays current queue lengths.
